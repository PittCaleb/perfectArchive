Beta Test Area

Welcome, Beta Testers!

We are currently developing a way for fans to experience The Perfect Line gameplay directly on this site. Our goal is to create modes for you to play the Preliminary Rounds, the Fast Line, and the Final Line, either individually, against an AI opponent, or against other players in a shared online session.

The rollout of these features will be slow and methodical. Eventually, visitors should be able to play individual game elements or a full game from start to finish, complete with multiple leaderboards to track performance. We appreciate your patience and your invaluable feedback as we develop these new gameplay elements.

If you observe anything amiss or have a feature request, please email us at beta@theperfectline.info. If you would like to nominate someone to join the beta test, please forward their email to the same address.
