About This Site

The story behind the numbers.

Welcome to the Perfect Line Archives, a passion project created by dedicated fans for fellow enthusiasts of the show. This site is a tribute to one of the most exciting game shows on television, born from a desire to dive deeper into the gameplay that captivates us. Please note, this is an unofficial fan site and has no affiliation with The Perfect Line, Game Show Network, CBS Media Ventures, or any of their parent or subsidiary companies.

At our core, we are obsessed with statistics. We believe that beneath the surface of every question and every score, there's a fascinating world of patterns, trends, and probabilities waiting to be discovered. We created this archive as a way to meticulously track the action on screen, hoping to uncover insights into the game's structure. Is there an advantage to a certain podium? Does one round consistently prove more challenging than others? These are the kinds of questions we aim to explore.

This archive is our attempt to share that exploration with the wider fan community. We hope you enjoy browsing through the game results and the evolving statistics as much as we enjoy compiling them. Thank you for visiting, and we hope this site enhances your appreciation for the brilliant strategy and gameplay of The Perfect Line.
