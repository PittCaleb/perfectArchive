How the Game is Played

The Perfect Line challenges four contestants to place a series of items in the correct order. In the four preliminary rounds, players share a prize pot for correctly positioning items on the board. The top two players advance to a high-speed "Fast Line" round, with the winner moving on to the final challenge: perfectly matching five items to their retail prices to win their entire accumulated jackpot.
